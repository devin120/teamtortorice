Our Mission.

Welcome to Team Tortorice Wrestling! Whether you're a novice or an experienced competitor, our dedicated staff is committed to taking your athletic journey to the next level. At Team Tortorice, we go beyond just improving your performance on the mat; we also equip you with tools that can be applied to your daily life. Our aim is to prepare the next generation to face the world, not just their opponents, and we take pride in developing not only the physical skills needed for wrestling, but also the mental endurance required for success. Join us and experience the comprehensive approach to wrestling that Team Tortorice has to offer.

Our training program is designed around three core categories: technique, conditioning, and live wrestling. With a dynamic training regimen, we strive to keep our athletes excited and motivated for each upcoming practice. We believe in fostering mental strength and resilience, both in preparation for matches and post-match recovery. Our team is passionate about improving and growing alongside our athletes, and we look forward to the opportunity to meet new talent and work hard together.
