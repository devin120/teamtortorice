About

Devin comes to you with 20+ years of wrestling experience ranging in all three different styles of wrestling. Devin was a four-year varsity starter for the University of Wisconsin-Whitewater where he was a NCAA qualifier and team captain. During his collegiate years, Devin trained all over the country and world. He competed and trained with the Moldovan and Bulgarian National/Olympic team members going 8-0 in his campaign. In 2017 he trained at the Olympic training center in Colorado springs. Devin also has 7 years of previous coaching experience. He comes from coaching at a variety of private clubs and high schools, helping numerous athletes compete at the highest levels of youth and high school competition. He is currently coaching at Lakes community high school as the associate head coach.
